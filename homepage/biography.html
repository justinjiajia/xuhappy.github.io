Huanle Xu – Background and CV

My name is Huanle Xu. I received my BSc(Eng) degree from the Department of Information Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2012 and my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2016. My PhD supervisor is Wing Cheong Lau who is a professor in the department of Information Engineering. I am particularly interested in designing wonderful algorithms for real applications and practical systems using mathematical tools.
